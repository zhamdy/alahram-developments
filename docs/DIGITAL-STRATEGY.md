## Executive Summary

### The Opportunity

Al-Ahram for Development and Real Estate Investment is a Sadat City-based real estate developer with:
- **1,800 Facebook followers** built in 5 months (page created Oct 2024)
- **3 active construction projects**: Project 865, Project 868 (Zone 21), Project 76 (near Al-Reyada University)
- **Strong community trust**: Loyal followers, positive engagement, "Top Fan" badges
- **Zero web presence** beyond Facebook — no website, no Google listing, no Instagram

### The Gap

- **No website** — the single biggest digital gap. Every competitor except the weakest has one
- **No SEO** — losing every Google search to competitors
- **No hashtags, no CTAs, no Google Business Profile**
- **Inconsistent posting** — static photos with no text get zero engagement

### The Market

- Sadat City real estate is booming: **33% YoY price growth**
- Population tripling toward **2 million by 2030**
- Average apartment price: **~EGP 11,500/sqm** (1/4 of New Cairo prices)
- **50+ compounds** planned for the next decade
- **EGP 5+ billion** in active private investment

### The Competition

| Company | Digital Score | Website | Facebook |
|---------|-------------|---------|----------|
| **Al-Ahram (us)** | **3/10** | None | 1.8K |
| Al-Rehab | 8/10 | Professional | ~12-15K |
| Aqar Group | 7/10 | Professional | ~10K |
| Bussma | 6/10 | Basic | ~12K |
| Meamar Al Moez | 5/10 | None | ~6.5K |
| Beytak | 2/10 | None | ~2-5K |

### The Recommendation

Build a professional Angular SSR website in 3 phases:
- **Phase 1 (4 weeks)**: MVP — Homepage, About, Projects, Contact, Gallery
- **Phase 2 (6 weeks)**: Growth — Payment Plans, Construction Updates, Sadat Guide, Blog infrastructure
- **Phase 3 (6 weeks)**: Leadership — Full blog, Investors page, English toggle, advanced features

**Projected outcome**: Move from 3/10 to 9/10 digital score, becoming the #1 digital real estate brand in Sadat City.
