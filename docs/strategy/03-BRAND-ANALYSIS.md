# 03 — Brand Analysis

## Logo

### Description
- **Style**: Arabic calligraphic typography — "الأهـرام" in bold strokes
- **Sub-text**: "للتطـويـر و الإستثمـار العقـارى" (Arabic)
- **English tagline**: "For Investment And Real Estate Development"
- **Color**: Orange/amber primary, dark brown secondary
- **Background**: White (profile) / Transparent overlay on cover

### Assessment
| Aspect | Score | Notes |
|--------|-------|-------|
| Memorability | 7/10 | Distinctive Arabic calligraphy stands out |
| Readability | 6/10 | Arabic calligraphy is artistic but not instantly readable at small sizes |
| Versatility | 5/10 | Works on white backgrounds; needs responsive versions for different sizes |
| Professionalism | 7/10 | Conveys heritage and authority — appropriate for real estate |
| Modern appeal | 5/10 | Calligraphic style leans traditional; may need a modern companion mark |

### Recommendations
1. Create a simplified icon version for small sizes (favicon, app icon)
2. Develop a horizontal lockup version for website header
3. Create light-on-dark and dark-on-light variations
4. Consider a modern Arabic typeface companion for body text

**Assessment**: This watermark system is well-executed and provides consistent branding. It should be replicated on the website.

## Cover Photo Analysis

- **Content**: Modern building facade with glass and steel architecture
- **Logo**: Al-Ahram logo prominently overlaid on the left
- **Quality**: High resolution, professional appearance
- **Message**: Conveys modernity, professionalism, and urban development
- **Issue**: The building shown is NOT one of their projects — it appears to be a stock/reference image of a modern tower. This creates a disconnect with their actual construction photos showing ground-level residential buildings.

### Recommendation
Replace the cover photo with a drone shot of their actual Zone 21 construction site or a professional render of their completed project vision.
