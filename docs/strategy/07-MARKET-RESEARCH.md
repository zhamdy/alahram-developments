# 07 — Market Research

## Sadat City Real Estate Market Overview

### City Profile

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Governorate** | Monufia |
| **Distance from Cairo** | ~90 km (1-1.5 hours) |
| **Current population** | ~500,000 (estimated) |
| **Target population** | 2,000,000 by 2030 |
| **Industrial base** | 2,000+ factories |
| **Universities** | Al-Reyada University, Sadat City University |
| **Classification** | 4th generation Egyptian new city |

### Price Trends

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Average price/sqm** | ~EGP 11,500 |
| **YoY price growth** | ~33% |
| **Comparison to New Cairo** | ~1/4 the price |
| **Comparison to 6th of October** | ~1/3 the price |
| **Typical unit sizes** | 110-195 sqm |
| **Estimated unit prices** | EGP 1,265,000 — EGP 2,242,500 |

### Market Drivers

1. **Population growth**: Government target of tripling population creates sustained demand
2. **Industrial expansion**: 2,000+ factories draw workers who need permanent housing
3. **University growth**: Students and faculty create rental and purchase demand
4. **Cairo spillover**: Priced-out Cairo buyers look to satellite cities
5. **Government investment**: Infrastructure improvements (roads, utilities, services)
6. **New compounds**: 50+ compounds planned, signaling developer confidence

## Payment & Financing Landscape

### Typical Market Terms

| Term | Range |
|------|-------|
| **Down payment** | 10-30% |
| **Installment period** | Up to 10 years |
| **Monthly installment** | Varies by plan |
| **Delivery premium** | Common for near-completion units |

### Financing Options in the Market

1. **Developer installment plans** — most common (used by Al-Ahram)
2. **Bank mortgage (التمويل العقاري)** — growing but still limited
3. **Social housing fund** — for qualifying lower-income buyers
4. **Cash purchases** — investors and expatriates
