## Engagement Analysis

### Key Finding: Video vs. Static

| Content Type | Average Engagement | Notes |
|-------------|-------------------|-------|
| **Reels (with text)** | 50-113 reactions, 6-49 comments | High engagement, especially community content |
| **Photos (with text)** | 32-38 reactions, 15-26 comments | Moderate engagement |
| **Photos (no text)** | **0 reactions, 0 comments** | Complete failure — zero engagement |

**Conclusion**: Photos posted without any caption text get absolutely zero engagement. Video reels outperform static photos by 100x+.

### Top Performing Content

1. **Quran Competition Reel** — 31K views, 287 shares (community/CSR)
2. **Investment Value Reel** — 14K views, 94 reactions (sales messaging)
3. **Projects 865 & 868 Reel** — 6.6K views, 76 reactions (construction)

### Content That Fails
- Batch-uploaded construction photos with no caption text
- No hashtags = no discoverability
- No CTA = no conversion

## Tone of Voice

- **Language**: Egyptian colloquial Arabic (عامية مصرية)
- **Style**: Casual, warm, confident, community-oriented
- **Examples**:
  - "بنقولها" (we say it) — confident
  - "هنعلن" (we'll announce) — teasing/exciting
  - "بنبني" (we build) — action-oriented
  - "انتم جزء منه" (you're part of it) — inclusive
- **Formality**: Low to medium — appropriate for Facebook/social audience
- **Emojis**: Used sparingly (💪🏻 mostly)

## Arabic Language Quality

- **Good**: Natural Egyptian dialect, relatable, authentic
- **Needs improvement**: Some posts have minor grammar inconsistencies
- **Missing**: No formal Arabic (فصحى) version for official content
